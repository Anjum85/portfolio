About Me

Hi, I’m Zaarin Anjum, a UI/UX Designer based in Bangladesh. I hold a degree in Software Engineering, which enhances my design work by providing a strong understanding of the technical aspects of software and websites. This background enables me to prioritize simplicity and usability, ensuring that the solutions I design are both functional and user-friendly.

I specialize in designing mobile apps and websites with a user-centered approach. My expertise includes user research, wireframing, and prototyping, allowing me to create designs that are intuitive and impactful.

I gained valuable experience as a UI/UX Design Intern at Grameen Communications, where I worked for four months. During my time there, I contributed to designing websites, landing pages, and logos. This opportunity significantly influenced my design philosophy and refined my professional approach to creating user-focused designs.
